
Write an XSL stylesheet to convert a BookFile to a TeX pretty-print
format of book entries.  May need to modify a bit for the HJS
collection as these are physical Items rather than Works or
Expressions, in the FRBR notation.

Title
Year, place of publication, Publisher
Bibliographic description
References to other lists
Comments:
Reasons why the book is important and any publishing history.

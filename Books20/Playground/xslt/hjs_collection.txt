Harlan J. Smith book collection at the McDonald Observatory
8 Nov 2021

Catalogue books from Harlan J. Smith collection from the first
donation and the second donation using'ajbbooks'.  The IndexName
should be 'HJS', the VolumeNumber should be 1 or 2 depending on the
donation, the SectionNumber should be 1, the IndexNumber should be as
catalogued. The page number should be 1.

Comments should include a description of condition and any feature
in the book, notations, jacket, etc.

Add protective covers to any dust jackets.
